Are KPIs and Balanced Scorecards Bad for Business?

When we attempt to measure human behavior and performance with the same rigid metrics used for machines, we risk hurting the very business we try to optimize.

The Human Variable

We often apply a single KPI to an entire staff, assuming uniformity. But human output is not linear. The Financial Trap

Measuring a company solely by the Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and P&L is nearsighted. These documents show the past, not the potential.

Case Study: Amazon

For years, Amazon reinvested nearly all profits back into the business. On paper (P&L), they looked like they were barely surviving. In reality, they were building an empire.
